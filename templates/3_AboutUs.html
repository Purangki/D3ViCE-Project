Vision

     Video conferencing as a means for people to share and communicate with others on ideas and help them accomplish more even in the confines of their own homes.

Mission

     To make video conferencing immersive, safe and seamless as possible. Save resources such as time, money and effort both on the sponsor’s side and the participant’s side.

What is D3Vice?

D3ViCE stands for 3D Virtual Conference Environment developed by the FLAME Developers. It caters to businesses, schools and other organizations in need of our services.
